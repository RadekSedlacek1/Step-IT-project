Bill 2 Split

Simple Expense Sharing

Welcome to Bill 2 Split, the app that simplifies managing shared expenses and dividing costs among multiple users. This app helps you efficiently track payments, keep records of transactions, and manage relationships between friends, family, or colleagues.

Purpose of the App

Bill 2 Split is designed for anyone who needs a simple way to track shared expenses. Whether it's for a group trip, household budget, or work-related costs, this app allows you to easily create payments, add users, and keep track of who owes what.

Main Features

  • Payment and expense distribution management
  • Creating and tracking payments
  • Overview of cost-sharing between users
  • Quick and easy addition of payments
